Online reputation management shapes how your business appears across search results, reviews, and social platforms — and increasingly, how AI systems describe you when someone asks for a recommendation in your category.

## What does an ORM engagement typically include?

A solid ORM program includes review monitoring and response across Google and industry-specific platforms, proactive positive content generation, and monitoring of brand mentions across the web to catch and address issues early.

## Why does reputation now affect AI-generated answers too?

Generative AI models often draw on aggregated sentiment and third-party content when describing a business, which means inconsistent information or unaddressed negative reviews can shape how ChatGPT or Perplexity characterize your brand — tying ORM directly to our [GEO services](/services/geo-services).

## How is ORM different from PR?

PR focuses on proactively generating positive coverage and media relationships, while ORM is more defensive and monitoring-focused — though the two work best together for businesses serious about their public perception.
