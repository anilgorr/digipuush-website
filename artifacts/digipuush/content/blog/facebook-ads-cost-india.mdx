Facebook and Instagram ad costs in India vary widely by industry, audience, and objective — this guide gives realistic benchmarks so you can budget with confidence.

## What determines Facebook ad costs in India?

Cost is driven primarily by audience competition (how many advertisers are bidding for the same audience), campaign objective (awareness campaigns cost less per impression than conversion campaigns), and creative quality and relevance score.

## What should different industries expect to pay?

E-commerce and D2C brands typically see the most efficient costs due to broad, engaged audiences, while B2B and financial services categories face higher costs per click and per lead due to narrower, more competitive targeting.

## How can businesses reduce their cost per lead?

The most reliable lever is creative and landing page quality — well-targeted campaigns with weak landing pages waste spend on clicks that don't convert. Pairing paid campaigns with strong [website development](/services/website-development) consistently lowers cost per lead more than adjusting bids alone.
