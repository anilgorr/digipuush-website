The best AEO agency in India is the one that runs a transparent, measurable process for getting your brand cited by AI answer engines — not the one that promises guaranteed citations. Because AEO is a young discipline, the honest way to choose is by criteria and questions rather than a ranked list of names. This guide gives you both.

*Disclosure: Digipuush is our own AEO agency, so we have a clear interest here. We have written the criteria below to be useful even if you never work with us — apply them to any provider you evaluate, including us.*

## What criteria actually matter when choosing an AEO agency?

Five criteria separate a real AEO partner from a rebranded SEO shop. Use them to evaluate anyone, including us.

1. **A documented audit-to-measurement process.** They should be able to explain exactly how they audit your current AI visibility, restructure content, and measure citations — not just talk in generalities.
2. **Real citation tracking.** They should test a fixed set of your buyer questions across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews every month and show you the movement.
3. **AEO and SEO treated as one strategy.** The best providers understand that the same clean, structured, factual pages serve both AI citations and Google rankings.
4. **Honest expectations.** They should refuse to guarantee specific citations and instead guarantee a repeatable process, because AI engines are third-party systems that change constantly.
5. **Transparent pricing and reporting.** You should always know what you are paying for and see measurable progress each month.

## What questions should you ask before signing?

Before committing to any AEO agency, ask:

- "Can you show me how you track AI citations, and can I see a sample monthly report?"
- "Which engines do you monitor, and how often?"
- "How do AEO and SEO fit together in your approach?"
- "What happens in month one versus month six?"
- "What do you explicitly not promise?"

The quality of these answers tells you more than any award or client-logo wall.

## What are the warning signs of a weak AEO agency?

Just as important as knowing what to look for is knowing what to avoid. A few red flags reliably signal that an agency will underdeliver:

1. **Guaranteed citations.** Anyone promising a specific ChatGPT or Perplexity citation is either misinformed or dishonest — those systems are outside any agency's control.
2. **No citation tracking.** If they cannot show you how they measure AI visibility month to month, they are selling activity, not results.
3. **Rebranded SEO with no method change.** Some agencies simply relabelled their SEO service as "AEO" without changing how they work. Ask what they do differently for answer engines specifically.
4. **Opaque pricing.** Vague, "it depends" pricing with no clear package or deliverables usually means the scope will shift against you.
5. **No named authorship or expertise.** AEO is about credibility; an agency that cannot demonstrate its own is unlikely to build yours.

## In-house vs agency: which is right for you?

Some businesses wonder whether to build AEO capability in-house instead of hiring an agency. Both paths can work, and the right choice depends on your resources.

| Factor | In-house | Agency |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ramp-up time | Slow — AEO is a new skill | Fast — process already exists |
| Cost structure | Salaries and tooling | Monthly retainer |
| Breadth of expertise | Limited to your hires | Cross-client experience |
| Best for | Large teams with steady content needs | Most SMBs and growth-stage brands |

For most small and growth-stage Indian businesses, an agency is more cost-effective early on because the expertise and tooling already exist. Larger organisations with constant content demand may eventually bring parts of it in-house, often while retaining an agency for strategy and measurement.

## How do you compare AEO agencies fairly?

The fairest way to compare providers is to run them through the same evaluation rather than reacting to sales pitches. Send each shortlisted agency the same five questions from earlier, ask for a sample monthly report, and request a specific month-one plan for your site. Then score them on the five criteria — process, tracking, integrated strategy, honesty, and transparency — using the same scale.

This turns a fuzzy decision into a structured one. It also surfaces how each agency thinks: the strong ones will engage with your specific situation, while the weak ones will fall back on generic promises. Applied consistently, this method protects you from choosing on brand familiarity or price alone.

## Does the agency's location in India matter?

With AEO delivered almost entirely through content, schema, and reporting, physical location is rarely a real constraint — a Bangalore agency can serve a client in Delhi, Mumbai, or a smaller city just as effectively as one down the road. What matters far more is whether the agency understands your market and your buyers' language.

There is, however, a case for local knowledge. An agency familiar with the Indian market understands how buyers here phrase questions, what price expectations are realistic, and which local platforms and publications carry weight. For businesses targeting local or regional customers specifically, an agency that grasps "near me"-style intent and Indian buyer behaviour has an edge. Weigh market understanding heavily, and treat physical proximity as a minor convenience rather than a deciding factor.
