Digital marketing is the umbrella term for every channel businesses use to reach customers online — and in 2026, that umbrella has expanded well beyond Google search to include AI answer engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity.

## What channels make up digital marketing today?

The core channels are search engine optimization (SEO) for Google rankings, Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) for AI visibility, social media marketing, paid advertising, and email marketing. Most effective strategies combine several of these rather than relying on one.

## Why has AI answer engine visibility become part of digital marketing?

As more research shifts to conversational AI tools, being cited inside a ChatGPT or Perplexity answer has become as valuable as ranking on page one of Google — sometimes more so, because it's often the only answer the user sees. Read more in our guide to [AEO services](/services/aeo-services).
