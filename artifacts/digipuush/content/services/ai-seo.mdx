AI SEO is what happens when traditional search optimization, Answer Engine Optimization, and Generative Engine Optimization run as one coordinated strategy instead of three separate efforts. ## Why do businesses need one strategy instead of three?

Running SEO, AEO, and GEO as disconnected initiatives usually means duplicated content work, inconsistent facts across pages, and wasted budget. Because all three disciplines share the same technical foundation — crawlable pages, clear structure, authoritative content, accurate schema — Digipuush builds them together from a single content calendar and technical roadmap.
