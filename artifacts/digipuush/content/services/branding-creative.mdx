Your brand identity is the visual and verbal system that makes your business recognizable and trustworthy at a glance. Digipuush designs brand identities — from logo to full visual system — built to work consistently across your website, social channels, and marketing materials.

## What does the branding process look like?

We begin with a discovery phase to understand your positioning, audience, and competitors, then develop logo concepts and a supporting visual system covering color, typography, and imagery. Every deliverable is provided in the formats your team needs for web, social, and print.

## Why does branding matter for SEO and AI visibility?

A clear, consistent brand identity improves trust signals that both users and AI systems pick up on — from a well-branded, professional website (which supports [AEO](/services/aeo-services) and [GEO](/services/geo-services) credibility signals) to consistent presentation across social platforms.

## Do you offer video and animation as part of creative services?

Yes — our creative team produces short-form video, animated explainers, and social content assets alongside static brand deliverables, typically as an add-on to a branding or [social media marketing](/services/social-media-marketing) engagement.

## How is branding priced?

Branding projects are quoted individually based on scope. Contact us for a proposal tailored to your business.
