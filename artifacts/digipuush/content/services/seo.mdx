SEO is the practice of optimizing your website's technical structure, content, and authority so it ranks higher on Google for the searches your customers actually run. Digipuush combines technical audits, on-page optimization, and backlink building to grow organic traffic for Indian businesses.

## What's included in Digipuush's SEO service?

We start with a full technical audit covering site speed, mobile usability, crawlability, and indexing issues, then move to on-page optimization — titles, meta descriptions, header structure, internal linking — across your priority pages. We pair this with keyword research grounded in real search volume and intent, and a backlink strategy focused on relevant, authoritative domains rather than volume.

## How is Digipuush's SEO different from a generic SEO agency?

Every SEO engagement we run is built on the same technical foundation as our [AEO](/services/aeo-services) and [GEO](/services/geo-services) work, which means your content stays optimized for AI answer engines as a byproduct of good SEO — not as a separate, disconnected effort.
