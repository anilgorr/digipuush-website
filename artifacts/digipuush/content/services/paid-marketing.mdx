Paid marketing delivers immediate, measurable pipeline while your organic SEO, AEO, and GEO efforts compound over time. Digipuush runs Google Ads and Meta Ads campaigns for Indian businesses with a strict focus on cost per lead and return on ad spend.

## What does a paid marketing engagement include?

Every engagement starts with audience and competitor research, followed by campaign build-out across the right mix of Google Search, Display, YouTube, and Meta placements. We build or optimize landing pages for conversion, then run continuous testing on creative, targeting, and bidding strategy.

## How does paid marketing fit alongside SEO and AI visibility work?

Paid campaigns give you immediate visibility and lead flow while organic channels — [SEO](/services/seo), [AEO](/services/aeo-services), and [GEO](/services/geo-services) — build compounding visibility over the following months. Most clients run paid and organic together rather than choosing one.

## Do you manage ad spend or just the management fee?

Ad spend is billed directly by the platforms (Google, Meta) to your account; Digipuush charges a separate management fee for strategy, execution, and optimization.

## How is paid marketing priced?

Management fees are scoped based on ad spend and platform complexity. Contact us for a proposal tailored to your budget and goals.
