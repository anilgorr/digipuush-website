Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is how your brand gets quoted directly inside ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews — not just ranked on page one. Digipuush restructures your content, adds machine-readable schema, and builds the kind of clear, factual answers AI systems trust enough to cite by name.

## Why does AEO matter for businesses in India right now?

Search behavior in India is shifting fast. A growing share of B2B and high-consideration purchase research now starts with a conversational question typed into ChatGPT or Perplexity rather than Google. If your competitor's content is structured to be quotable and yours isn't, they get cited — and you get skipped, regardless of how well you rank in classic search.

## How does Digipuush's AEO process work?

We start with an AI visibility audit across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini for your core buyer questions, then rebuild priority pages around three pillars: direct-answer openers that satisfy a query in the first 40-60 words, structured FAQ and comparison sections that map to real questions, and Organization, Service, and FAQPage schema markup so AI crawlers can parse facts unambiguously. Every page ships with clear authorship and up-to-date "last modified" signals, both of which AI systems weigh when deciding what to trust.
