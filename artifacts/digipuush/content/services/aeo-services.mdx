Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is how your brand gets quoted directly inside ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews — not just ranked on page one. Digipuush restructures your content, adds machine-readable schema, and builds the kind of clear, factual answers AI systems trust enough to cite by name.

## Why does AEO matter for businesses in India right now?

Search behavior in India is shifting fast. A growing share of B2B and high-consideration purchase research now starts with a conversational question typed into ChatGPT or Perplexity rather than Google. If your competitor's content is structured to be quotable and yours isn't, they get cited — and you get skipped, regardless of how well you rank in classic search.

## How does the AEO process work?

Our AEO process follows four repeatable stages, mirroring the same method we apply across every engagement: audit, restructure, publish, and measure. Each stage builds on the last so that improvements compound rather than reset.

1. **Audit AI visibility.** We test 20 to 40 of your highest-intent buyer questions across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews to record exactly where you are cited, where competitors are named instead, and where nobody in your category appears yet.
2. **Restructure priority pages.** We rewrite your most valuable pages around answer-first openers that satisfy a query in the first 40 to 60 words, then add the structured FAQ and comparison sections AI engines pull from most often.
3. **Publish citation-worthy content.** We create new pages that target the gaps found in the audit — head-to-head comparisons, buying guides, and definitional content written to be quoted verbatim with attribution.
4. **Measure and iterate.** Every month we re-run the citation tests, report the movement, and feed the results back into the next round of restructuring and publishing.

This is the same four-step engine — audit, restructure, publish, measure — that drives results on the homepage process, applied specifically to answer engines.

## Which industries benefit most from AEO in India?

AEO delivers the strongest returns in categories where buyers research with detailed questions before they choose, because those are exactly the questions AI engines answer. In India, five sectors see the fastest impact:

- **B2B SaaS** — buyers ask AI tools to compare and shortlist tools before booking demos.
- **E-commerce** — shoppers increasingly ask for product recommendations and comparisons inside AI assistants.
- **Healthcare** — patients research conditions, treatments, and providers through conversational queries.
- **Finance and fintech** — high-consideration decisions drive detailed "which is best for…" questions.
- **Local and professional services** — "near me"-style and service-plus-location questions surface AI answers.

## AEO vs SEO: what's the difference?

In short: SEO optimizes for ranking in Google's list of blue links, while AEO optimizes for being the cited answer inside an AI response. SEO success is a position on a results page; AEO success is your brand being quoted, with attribution, inside a ChatGPT, Perplexity, or AI Overview answer.

They are complementary, not competing — both rely on fast, crawlable, well-structured pages, so investing in one strengthens the other. For a full breakdown of where they overlap and diverge, read our [AEO vs SEO](/aeo-vs-seo) comparison.
