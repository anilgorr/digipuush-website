A website built for 2026 has to satisfy three audiences at once: human visitors, Google's crawler, and AI crawlers like GPTBot and PerplexityBot that can't execute JavaScript. Digipuush builds fast, server-rendered websites that work for all three from launch.

## Why does website architecture matter for AI visibility?

Many AI crawlers do not execute client-side JavaScript, which means content rendered entirely in the browser is often invisible to them. Digipuush builds on frameworks that generate real HTML on the server, so every page's content, headings, and schema markup are present in the raw response an AI crawler receives.

## What's included in a website development project?

Every project includes responsive design, SEO-informed page copy, technical SEO setup (sitemap, robots directives, schema markup), performance optimization, and a CMS or content structure your team can update without a developer.
