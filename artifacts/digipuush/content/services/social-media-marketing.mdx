Social media marketing builds the audience and brand presence that supports every other growth channel — including the trust signals that feed into AI answer engines. Digipuush runs organic content and paid campaigns across Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook for Indian brands.

## What does a social media marketing retainer include?

Each retainer includes a content calendar and creative production, community management, and paid promotion where budget allows, all tied to measurable goals like engagement rate, follower growth, and qualified lead volume rather than vanity metrics alone.

## Do you run influencer campaigns?

Yes — for brands where influencer partnerships fit the audience, we identify and manage relevant creators as part of a broader social strategy, rather than treating influencer marketing as a standalone tactic.

## How does social media marketing connect to SEO and AI visibility?

A consistently active, well-branded social presence reinforces the trust and authority signals that support both traditional SEO and our [AEO](/services/aeo-services)/[GEO](/services/geo-services) work — AI systems increasingly reference how a brand shows up across the web, not just on its own site.

Pricing depends on platform count and content volume. Contact us for a scoped proposal.
