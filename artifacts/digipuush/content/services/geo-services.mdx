Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) makes sure ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity describe your brand accurately — and mention it at all — when someone asks for a recommendation or comparison in your category. Digipuush builds the structured, fact-dense content these models are trained to trust and quote.

## Why do generative AI engines skip most business websites?

Generative models favor content that is unambiguous, well-labeled, and backed by structured data. Most business websites bury their core facts in marketing language, images, or JavaScript-rendered widgets that AI crawlers can't reliably parse. GEO fixes this by making your key facts — pricing, service scope, location, credentials — extractable in plain, crawlable HTML.

## What does Digipuush actually change on a website for GEO?

We rewrite key pages with explicit, fact-first paragraphs, add comparison tables and FAQ sections addressing the exact way people phrase questions to AI assistants, and implement Organization, Service, Person, and FAQPage schema so every fact on the page has a machine-readable counterpart. We also monitor how competitors are represented in the same AI outputs so we can close specific visibility gaps.

## How does GEO fit with SEO and AEO?

GEO, AEO, and traditional SEO share a technical foundation — crawlable, fast, well-structured pages — but differ in what they optimize for: rankings (SEO), direct-answer citations (AEO), and broader generative representation (GEO).
