Fix it at the source: keep the title.template: '%s | Digipuush' in the root layout, and strip "| Digipuush" from every page-level and MDX frontmatter title that contains it.
After the fix, grep the built HTML output for "Digipuush | Digipuush" — must return zero matches across all 30 pages.
